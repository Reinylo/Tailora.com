About Our Team

We are a passionate group of computing students from Asia Pacific University (APU), brought together by our shared vision of revolutionizing the fashion and tailoring industry through technology. Our diverse academic backgrounds provide us with a comprehensive skill set to tackle complex challenges.

We've combined our classroom knowledge with real-world experience to create Tailora - an innovative platform that bridges the gap between traditional tailoring and modern technology.

Our commitment to excellence, collaborative spirit, and shared passion for technology-driven solutions make us not just classmates, but partners in innovation. Together, we're dedicated to making Tailora a game-changer in the fashion industry while preparing ourselves for successful careers in the tech world.
