How to Measure Yourself

📏

Chest/Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

⚡

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, which is the narrowest part of your torso.

🎯

Hip

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 7-9 inches below your waist.

👔

Shoulder

Measure from the edge of one shoulder to the edge of the other shoulder across your back.

💪

Sleeve

Measure from your shoulder point down to your wrist, with your arm slightly bent.

📐

Inseam

Measure from your crotch down to your ankle, along the inside of your leg.

Fitting Tips

📊

Accurate Measurements

Consult Your Tailor

When in doubt, discuss your measurements and preferences directly with your chosen tailor.
