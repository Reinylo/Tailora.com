Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete an order?

+

The completion time varies depending on the complexity of your design and the tailor's schedule. Most orders are completed within 1-3 weeks. You can check estimated completion times when selecting a tailor.

What if I'm not satisfied with the final product?

+

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your order, you can request revisions or a refund within 7 days of delivery. Our customer service team will work with you and the tailor to resolve any issues.

How accurate is the AR try-on feature?

+

Our AR technology provides a good approximation of how garments will look and fit. However, it's a visualization tool and actual results may vary. We recommend discussing fit preferences directly with your tailor for the best results.

Can I cancel or modify my order after placing it?

+

You can cancel or modify your order within 24 hours of placing it, provided the tailor hasn't started working on it yet. After work begins, modifications may incur additional charges, and cancellations may only result in partial refunds.

How do I provide my measurements?

+

You can enter your measurements manually using our size guide, or upload photos/videos following our measurement instructions. Many tailors also offer in-person measurement services for an additional fee.
