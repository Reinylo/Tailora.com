Our Story

Tailora was born from a simple observation: finding a skilled tailor shouldn't be difficult, and creating custom clothing shouldn't be limited by geography or technology. Our founders, passionate about both fashion and technology, envisioned a platform where creativity meets craftsmanship.

Starting with a small team of designers and developers, we've built a comprehensive ecosystem that empowers tailors to showcase their skills, helps customers visualize their designs with AR technology, and connects communities through the shared love of well-made clothing.

Today, Tailora serves thousands of customers and hundreds of tailors across multiple cities, making custom clothing more accessible, sustainable, and enjoyable than ever before.
