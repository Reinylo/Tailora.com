About Tailora

Revolutionizing the tailoring industry through technology

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Tailora emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern technology. Our founders, passionate about both fashion and innovation, recognized that the tailoring industry needed a digital transformation that would benefit both artisans and customers alike.

What started as a small startup has grown into a comprehensive platform that connects skilled tailors with customers worldwide, offering everything from simple alterations to bespoke creations. We've revolutionized the way people think about custom clothing by making it accessible, transparent, and enjoyable.
