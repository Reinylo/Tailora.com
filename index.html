Frequently Asked Question

Can I use more than one fabric type in one piece?

Yes. you can adjust the fabric type for each part of your design individually

Can I ask to use my own fabric?

Yes. you may chat with the tailor to use your own fabric

What is the main use for the AI assistant?

AI assistant suggest you design ideas based on your preferance, trends and Occasion. As well as suitable fabrics. Can also make you a full design with a single prompt. and even more!

What happens if a tailor cancels my order?

In the event of a cancellation by a tailor, you will receive a full refund. Our team will also assist you in finding an alternative tailor that suits your preferences.
